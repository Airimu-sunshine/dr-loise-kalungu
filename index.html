About Dr. Kalungu

Healing rooted in
science & empathy

"Every person carries an inner wisdom. My work is to help you find and trust yours."

Dr. Loise Kalungu is a licensed Counselling psychologist with over a decade of experience supporting individuals, couples, and families through some of life's most challenging moments. Trained in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, trauma-informed care, and mindfulness-based approaches, she brings both scientific rigour and genuine warmth to every session.

She works with adults and adolescents navigating anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, stress and personal growth - always with deep respect for each client's unique background and goals.

Questions

Frequently
asked

Everything you need to know before taking the first step. Still have questions? Don't hesitate to reach out.

Have a specific question? Email directly at hello@loise-kalungu.co.ke or call +254 711 270 620.
What happens in the first session?
+
The first session is a consultation — a relaxed conversation where we discuss what brings you to therapy, what you hope to achieve, and whether we're a good fit. There's no pressure and no judgement. You set the pace entirely.
How long does therapy take?
+
It varies significantly by person and presenting concern. Short-term CBT typically runs 8–16 sessions. Others benefit from longer-term work. We assess and review regularly so you always know where you stand.
Is everything I share kept confidential?
+
Absolutely. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of ethical practice. The only exceptions are situations involving risk of serious harm to yourself or others — and even then, you'll always be informed first.
Do you offer online sessions?
+
Yes. Individual therapy sessions are available both in-person (Nairobi) and online via a secure, confidential video platform. Couples and family sessions are best done in-person but exceptions can be made.
What are your fees?
+
Individual sessions are KES 2,000–3,000. Couples and pair sessions are KES 3,500– 5,500. A limited number of reduced-fee slots are available for those with financial constraints. Please enquire confidentially.
Do I need a referral from a doctor?
+
No referral is needed. You can book directly. If you've been referred by a psychiatrist or GP, do bring any relevant notes — it helps provide the best care from session one.
